Commonly Asked Questions

Excellent Question, I must say! Our products are free of any by-products such as Monosodium Glutamate ("MSG"). We do happen to include some flavor enhancers, but those are a trade secret.

For any issues the customer notices, please do not hesitate to send us a message from our Contact Us page. Our drug testing policy follows the standards of any organization, but our policy is internal.
